Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum fee for online doctor consultation?

The minimum fee varies depending on the doctor's expertise and availability. Please check the consultation fees listed on the doctor's profile.

Is online doctor consultation legal in India?

Yes, online doctor consultations are legal in India. The Medical Council of India has issued guidelines for telemedicine practice.

What are the advantages of online doctor consultation?

Online consultations offer convenience, accessibility, and time-saving benefits. You can consult doctors from the comfort of your home, avoiding travel and waiting times.

Is my consultation private with my doctor?

Yes, your consultation is completely private and confidential. We adhere to strict privacy policies to protect your personal and medical information.

How qualified are the doctors?

Our verification process for every MediBuddy doctor is comprehensive. Each doctor is verified by our team, manually and electronically, validating all the relevant documents pertaining to their qualifications and experience.

How long can I consult with a doctor?

The duration of the consultation depends on the complexity of your health issue. Typically, consultations last between 10 to 30 minutes.

What happens if I miss an appointment?

If you miss an appointment, you can reschedule it through our platform. However, repeated missed appointments may incur a fee. Please refer to our cancellation policy for more details.
